About Us

IRA Foods & Beverages is a Kolhapur-based enterprise dedicated to producing high-quality, authentic organic products including jaggery, spices, and turmeric. With a focus on purity, sustainability, and health, we empower farmers and bring India's rich food heritage to the world.
